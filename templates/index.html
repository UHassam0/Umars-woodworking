Welcome to Umar's Woodworking, a website where you can discover the art and craft of woodworking. My name is Umar and I am a woodworker based in Birmingham, UK. I have been passionate about woodworking since I was a child and I love to create beautiful and functional pieces of furniture, decor, and accessories from wood.

On this website, you can see some of my projects that showcase my skills and style. You can also submit and manage requests for a booking if you are interested in hiring me for your woodworking needs. Whether you want a custom-made table, a bookshelf, a cutting board, or anything else made of wood, I can help you turn your vision into reality.

I work with different types of wood, such as oak, pine, walnut, and cherry, and I use various techniques, such as carving, turning, joinery, and finishing. I pay attention to every detail and ensure that each piece is made with quality and care. I also follow the best practices of environmental sustainability and use recycled or reclaimed wood whenever possible.

If you have any questions or comments, please feel free to contact me through the website or by email. I would love to hear from you and discuss your woodworking ideas. Thank you for visiting Umar's Woodworking and I hope you enjoy browsing through my website.
